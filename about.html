Increasing Your Creativity

Blueprint is a powerful tool for developers that quickly generates reusable templates for code, Blender models, and lighting effects (VFX), streamlining your workflow.

Mission

Blueprint empowers game developers with a free plugin that simplifies asset creation on the LuaR engine through tailored templates. Our mission is to democratize high-quality tools for developers of all skill levels.

Vision

Blueprint envisions accessible game development for all. We aim to be the leading resource for developers, fostering a collaborative community that transforms the game creation landscape.

The Blueprint Story

Blueprint was born from a passion for making game development more accessible and efficient for creators of all backgrounds. Our founders, a diverse group of seasoned developers and artists, recognized the common challenges faced by both newcomers and experienced developers in creating high-quality game assets.

Frustrated by the complexity of existing tools and the steep learning curve associated with them, they envisioned a solution that would streamline the asset creation process. Their goal was to allow developers to focus on their creativity rather than getting bogged down by technical hurdles.

The Plugin

Blueprint is a revolutionary plugin designed to simplify game asset creation for developers using the LuaR engine. By generating fast, tailored templates based on individual coding styles and best practices, Blueprint empowers creators to produce high-quality assets with ease. This means less time spent on repetitive tasks and more time dedicated to innovation and creativity.

With seamless integration into Blender, Blueprint offers a rich library of free templates that help streamline the design process. These templates are not only easy to use but also come with minimal technical requirements, making them accessible for developers at all skill levels. Our user-friendly interface allows for quick customization, ensuring that each asset can be tailored to fit the unique vision of your game.

Additionally, Blueprint supports best coding practices, which helps developers learn and apply effective techniques as they create. This educational aspect empowers users to improve their skills while producing professional-grade assets. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting out, Blueprint is here to enhance your workflow and elevate your game development experience.
